# Team glossary — template (you own this)

This is the **human-owned** term sheet the `meeting-notes` skill checks before
expanding any jargon — it is the meeting domain's *semantic layer*, the same
idea as the data-analyst's. The product ships this *template*; your filled-in
copy lives in **your** space and is treated as **your data** — a data reset
clears it, the shipped template does not.

Why human-owned: a term's expansion is a fact about *your team*, not something
a model can infer. The skill never invents an expansion — unknown terms come
back flagged "needs confirmation", and once you confirm one, it becomes a
one-line entry here. Watching this file grow from 0 to dozens of terms is the
honest growth metric; new teammates can be handed a copy on day one.

Keep entries one line each; add the date so stale terms are visible.
